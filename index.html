A crucial conversation about mental health, warning signs, and how communities can come together to prevent suicide. Resources, hope, and practical steps for those struggling or supporting someone in crisis.

Meet Your Host

Chaplain Bracey Goodwin, IV

Chaplain Bracey brings a unique perspective shaped by service, struggle, and faith. As an ordained chaplain, U.S. Air Force veteran, and safety professional with over 10 years of experience, he understands both the practical and spiritual dimensions of life's challenges.

Author of the memoir "Why Didn't I Listen? A Journey Through Trauma, Faith, and Redemption," Bracey shares his own story of transformation to help others find hope and healing.
